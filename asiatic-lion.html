Asiatic Lion

Population

The asiatic lion is one of the rarest lion species in the world with only 600 left in the wild.

Size

The asiatic lion is the smallest species of lion, weighing from 110 - 190kg, and measuring 1.4 - 2.5m in length and 80cm - 1.2m in heigh.

Location

The asiatic lion can be found in the Gujarat region of India.
