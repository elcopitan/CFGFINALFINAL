Mangrove Hummingbird

Population

The mangrove hummingbird population is steadily declining. Currently, there's thought to be between 2,500 - 10,000 of them in existence.

Size

The mangrove hummingbird weighs aroung 4.5g and measures between 9.5-11cm.

Location

The mangrove hummingbird can be found in Costa Rica.
