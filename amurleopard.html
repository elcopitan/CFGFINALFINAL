Amur Leopard

Population

The amur leopard is thought to be the rarest big cat in the world today, with only 100 left in the wild.

Size

The amur leopard can weight between 25-48kg, and grow to a height of up to 78cm and a length of up to 1.4m.

Location

The amur leopard is native to Russia, China, and the wilds of North Korea.
